Topics, Not Flashcards

Add entire topics like "Brachial Plexus" or "Krebs Cycle" — not individual cards. Study your notes, then mark it done.

Smart Rescheduling

Miss a revision? No problem. Revisio reschedules from today, not from the original missed date — unlike every other app.

Done / Postpone

Two simple actions per topic. Mark it done or push it to tomorrow. Clean, distraction-free revision tracking.

Calendar View

See exactly what's coming up. Visual dots on each date show how many revisions are scheduled so you can plan ahead.

Streak Tracking

Track your revision streak and total topics completed. Watch your consistency build over time.

Private and Secure

Your revision data is private and synced securely across all your devices. Study anywhere, anytime.

How It Works

Simple by design.

1

Add a topic

Type the topic name and subject — "Anatomy", "Biochemistry", whatever fits your course. Revisio schedules all 6 revision dates automatically.

2

Revise when reminded

Open the app and see exactly what needs revision today. Go through your notes, then mark it Done.

3

Build long-term memory

Each revision at the right interval strengthens the memory trace. By Day 60, the topic is locked in for the long term.
